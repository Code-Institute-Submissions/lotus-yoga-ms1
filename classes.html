Classes

Hatha
These 90min classes covering pranayama (breath work), asanas (poses) and short meditation at the end of the practice will leave you charged for the rest of the week. Suitable for all levels.

Slow Flow
These classes are great for anyone new to yoga. Also it is great for those who want to slow down and deepen their practice

Yogalates
Incorporates different elements of Pilates and yoga. Focusing on core strengthening and increasing flexibility. Suitable for all levels.

Meditation
We run different guided meditations to increase mindfulness and bring you into the present moment. All levels welcome.
